The book in a nutshell

The success of a small group is often judged by their outcomes, such as the numbers of members attending regularly, their spiritual practices around worship and prayer, their ability to care for each other, and their capacity to reach out to their community.

Little attention has been given to the power of conversation that happens within their meetings. This book is a personal testimony of how a straight-forward five-stage coaching framework, supported by seven principles and examples of great questions, can lead to your small group members thriving out of their godly identity and moving towards their ultimate calling of God in their lives.

What is a Powerful Conversation?

A powerful conversation is an intentional conversation which supports a group of people to work through their thinking/challenges, using a series of coaching type questions, while collectively listening to the Holy Spirit and praying together.

What are the stages & principles of a Powerful Conversation?

The stages of the framework are rooted in ideas of critical thinking and enquiry-based learning and can be used to support your group to delve deeper into a conversation relating to the bible, a sermon, a video, and a book etc. Its 5 stages, check-in, share, explore, consider and check-out, will guide and transform your group members’ discussion. Combined with its 7 underpinning principles, it will help your group members to go deeper with their faith and thrive out of their godly identity.

The 5 Step Coaching Framework

Step 1: Check-in

Begin with an intentional moment to connect and assess where group members are emotionally and spiritually.

Step 2: Share

Create a safe space for group members to share their thoughts, experiences, and insights.

Step 3: Explore

Dive deeper through powerful questions that encourage reflection and discovery.

Step 4: Consider

Help the group consider the implications and applications of their insights.

Step 5: Check-out

Close with commitment to action, prayer, and accountability for what was shared.

The 7 Principles of a Powerful Conversation

  1. Every member is uniquely gifted to serve God
  2. New learning is built on existing knowledge
  3. Good questions enable others to think more deeply and learn creatively
  4. Listening is a key foundation for good conversations
  5. The Holy Spirit is already present to guide
  6. Insights need to be aligned with action
  7. Accountability and prayer create momentum
